# Artwork provenance

The primary artwork in this directory was generated specifically for Quadrant Wars with the built-in OpenAI image generation tool. `river_mask.png` is a locally derived alpha mask of `battlefield.png`. No third-party game artwork is bundled.

## Final prompt set

- `battlefield.png`: Premium hand-painted 2.5D top-down fantasy battlefield; broad emerald grassland, dirt paths, rocks, flowers, streams and bridges; open playable center; bright late-morning light; terrain only; no UI, units, buildings, borders, labels, logos or watermark.
- `menu_battlefield.png`: Cinematic wide highland battlefield with four distant red, blue, green and gold miniature armies converging on a fortress; quiet dark space on the left for controls; golden-hour lighting; no text, UI, logos or watermark.
- `soldier.png`: One neutral medieval infantry miniature with sword, shield, steel armor and leather; high three-quarter top-down view; polished hand-painted mobile strategy sprite; crisp silhouette; no faction color, scenery, text or watermark.
- `worker.png`: One neutral medieval worker miniature with pickaxe and grain sack; high three-quarter top-down view; polished hand-painted mobile strategy sprite; crisp silhouette; no faction color, scenery, text or watermark.
- `queen.png`: One neutral warrior queen miniature with crown, ceremonial staff and practical ivory/gold/steel armor; high three-quarter top-down view; polished hand-painted mobile strategy sprite; no faction color, scenery, text or watermark.
- `fortress.png`: One compact neutral stone command keep with two towers, battlements and wooden gate; high three-quarter isometric view; polished hand-painted mobile strategy building sprite; no flags, faction colors, scenery, text or watermark.
- `fortress_western.png`: Existing fortress as style/camera reference; Western Highland keep with rugged dark fieldstone, stout round towers, slate conical roofs, iron braces and restrained lion shields; same readable gate and footprint; flat `#ff00ff` background; no flags, scenery, text or watermark.
- `fortress_northern.png`: Existing fortress as style/camera reference; Northern River Kingdom keep with pale granite, tall octagonal towers, steep teal roofs, copper trim and wave/knot carvings; same readable gate and footprint; flat `#ff00ff` background; no flags, scenery, text or watermark.
- `fortress_forest.png`: Existing fortress as style/camera reference; Forest Realm keep with moss-touched stone, dark timber, green curved roofs, leaf carvings, wooden finials and restrained ivy; same readable gate and footprint; flat `#ff00ff` background; no flags, scenery, text or watermark.
- `fortress_sun.png`: Existing fortress as style/camera reference; Sun Court citadel with warm ivory limestone, stepped towers, gold-bronze domes, blue ceramic inlay and sunburst reliefs; same readable gate and defensive footprint; flat `#ff00ff` background; no flags, scenery, text or watermark.
- `soldier_animation.png`: Using the existing Soldier as the exact character reference, create one clean 4-column by 2-row sprite sheet. Top row is a four-key walk cycle with alternating planted feet and natural shield/sword counter-motion. Bottom row is a four-key sword attack cycle: anticipation, wind-up, strike/impact and recovery. Fixed three-quarter top-down game camera, identical armor and proportions in every frame, isolated poses on flat `#ff00ff`, no text, UI, scenery or watermark.
- `worker_animation.png`: Using the existing Worker as the exact character reference, create one clean 4-column by 2-row sprite sheet. Top row is a four-key walk cycle carrying the pickaxe and grain sack. Bottom row is a four-key pickaxe work cycle: raise, swing, ground impact and recovery. Fixed three-quarter top-down game camera, identical clothing and proportions in every frame, isolated poses on flat `#ff00ff`, no text, UI, scenery or watermark.
- `queen_animation.png`: Using the existing Queen as the exact character reference, create one clean 4-column by 2-row sprite sheet. Top row is a poised four-key regal walk cycle with cape follow-through. Bottom row is a four-key staff attack cycle: anticipation, wind-up, forward strike and recovery. Fixed three-quarter top-down game camera, identical crown, armor, staff and proportions in every frame, isolated poses on flat `#ff00ff`, no text, UI, scenery or watermark.

The sprite/building sources and animation sheets were generated against a flat `#ff00ff` chroma-key background, then converted to alpha PNGs with the image generation skill's chroma-key removal helper. Runtime animation extraction uses connected components rather than rigid cell crops, preserving wide swords, pickaxes and capes at frame boundaries. The river mask was extracted from blue-dominant water pixels, expanded slightly and feathered so procedural highlights remain inside the painted river banks.
